About Arc AI

Founded on the principle that technology should serve humanity, Arc AI bridges the gap between advanced AI and real-world social problems. We are a collective of thinkers, developers, and policy experts dedicated to building data-driven solutions that are ethical, sustainable, and scalable. Our focus is on creating tangible tools that empower communities, improve governance, and foster a more equitable and sustainable world.

Our Approach: How AI Creates Social Tech

Artificial Intelligence is more than just technology; it's a powerful tool for understanding complex problems and building scalable solutions. Here's how we leverage it.

Data-Driven Insights

AI allows us to analyze vast datasets—from satellite imagery to public surveys—to uncover hidden patterns and identify the root causes of social challenges, ensuring our interventions are targeted and effective.

Predictive Modeling

We build models that can forecast future trends, such as climate risks, disease outbreaks, or resource shortages. This enables proactive planning and early intervention by policymakers and communities.

Accessible Tools

Our goal is to create user-friendly civic-tech platforms. AI powers the backend of these tools, making complex data accessible and empowering citizens and organizations to make informed decisions.

AI for Impact Projects

DigiAddress: Building Inclusive Cities

Millions in informal settlements lack a formal address, barring them from essential services. DigiAddress uses AI and Plus Codes to generate unique digital addresses, making the invisible visible and ensuring every citizen can access their rights.

NatureYatri: Making Transportation Sustainable

This platform helps users understand and compare the carbon emissions of different transport modes in Indian cities. By visualizing environmental impact, NatureYatri empowers citizens and policymakers to make informed, low-carbon travel decisions.
